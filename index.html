What the heck is a Serval?








  • Servals are medium-sized wild cats commonly found in various regions across northern Africa, but south of the Sahara desert. [1]
  • Servals are the only member of the genus Leptailurus, belonging to the Felidae family within the animal kingdom, making them cousins to both large cats such as lions and tigers, as well as common house cats.[2]
  • Servals are carnivorous ambush predators, often relying on stealth and their quickness to catch prey.[4]
  • Although they may look adorable, servals can be dangerous if threatened.[4]
  • Although not a direct threat to humans due to their shy nature, a hungry adult serval could probably eat a human if necessary.[3]
